History

How we started?

TechHub has a long history of importing computers and components which dates back to 1943, as my grandfather Sahil was the first person to introduce computer in nepal which was ENIAC. And for the last 2 generation our family has been involved in laptop and pc imports.and after 60 years of importing, we have finally achieved the imposiible.Nepal is the first third world contry able to produce and manufracture electronic components like laptops and Pc.And althought we haven't entered the global market we hope to compete with other brands .

Vision

What is our aim?

The only main goal or the vision of our company is simply to reach the global market by making quality products. we want to show the world that even a third world country like Nepal can achieve greatness through our hard work and dedication. And in this process we can also develop our nation as Nepali engineers, designer,programmers are getting employment in their own nation. So make all of this possible our company has made various donations to schools to create technical manpower in our own country.

Company

The Company

Our company has 10 branches altogether in which 8 are in Nepal and two are in India. 8 branches in Nepal are bulit in 8 major cities like kathmandu, bitrathnagar, pokhara, dhangadi,lalitpur,bhaktapur and lumbini.

We haven't entered the global market yet but we have made our name in india. we have also sold our products in pakistan and bhutan. So for our company and our nation to grow we would like you citizen of Nepal to invest into our future by using techHub.

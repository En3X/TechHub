History
TechHub has a long history of importing computers and components which dates back to 1943, as my grandfather Sahil was the first person to introduce computer in nepal which was ENIAC. And for the last 2 generation our family has been involved in laptop and pc imports.and after 60 years of importing, we have finally achieved the imposiible.Nepal is the first third world contry able to produce and manufracture electronic components like laptops and Pc.And althought we haven't entered the global market we hope to compete with other brands like Dell,asus etc.

Vision

What We Believe
The only main goal or the vision of our company is simply to reach the global market by making quality products. we want to show the world that even a third world country like Nepal can achieve greatness through our hard work and dedication. and in this process we can also develop our nation as Nepali engineers, designer,programmers are getting employment in their own nation. So make all of this possible our company has made various donations to schools to create technical manpower in our own country.
